About ClothCare

Spreading warmth and hope, one donation at a time.

Our Story

ClothCare started with a simple belief — no one should face the cold without warmth. We bridge the gap between generous donors and those in need through a transparent, easy-to-use platform.

From collecting old clothes to collaborating with verified NGOs, every step is driven by compassion and accountability. Together, we turn unused fabrics into symbols of care and comfort.

Our journey began with a small initiative but has now grown into a community movement that connects hearts across distances — transforming unused clothes into a message of hope.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To connect compassionate people with underprivileged communities, ensuring every piece of clothing finds its rightful owner and purpose.

Vision

A future where no one suffers from lack of clothing — where kindness becomes the thread that unites humanity.
